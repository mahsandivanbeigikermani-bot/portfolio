Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be in the EU for you to work with me?
No. I work with founders remotely. My regulatory expertise covers EU and UK markets, which are the most stringent globally—if your product is compliant there, it is likely compliant in most other markets. I also advise on US, GCC, and ASEAN requirements.
I already have a lab. Can you still help me?
Yes—and this is often where I add the most value. Having a lab doesn't mean having a complete brief. I ensure your lab receives a specification they can quote against and work from without costly back-and-forth.
Do you formulate the product yourself?
I provide the R&D strategy, ingredient selection, and full technical specification—the "blueprint." The physical prototype work is carried out by the lab under my technical direction. This gives you strategic independence and manufacturing-grade execution.
